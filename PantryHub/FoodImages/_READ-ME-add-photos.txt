HOW TO ADD FOOD PHOTOS TO PANTRYHUB
===================================

Drop your downloaded food photos straight into THIS folder (FoodImages).
Xcode will pick them up automatically — no dragging into Xcode needed.

RULES FOR EACH PHOTO
- File type: .jpg or .png
- Name it EXACTLY as listed below (all lowercase, hyphens, no spaces)
- Landscape (wide) photos look best, around 1200px wide or larger

PHOTOS NEEDED (12 recipes + 1 optional profile picture)

Breakfast
  french-toast            - a plate of french toast
  avocado-toast           - avocado toast
  berry-pancakes          - pancakes topped with berries

Lunch
  caesar-salad            - chicken caesar salad
  margherita-pizza        - margherita pizza
  buddha-bowl             - a veggie grain / buddha bowl

Dinner
  chicken-pad-thai        - chicken pad thai noodles
  spaghetti-bolognese     - spaghetti bolognese
  italian-lasagna         - a serving of lasagna

Snacks
  chocolate-chip-cookies  - chocolate chip cookies
  hummus-veggies          - hummus with veggie sticks
  fruit-yogurt-parfait    - a fruit & yogurt parfait

Optional
  profile                 - a photo of you for the greeting bar

Example final file name:  chicken-pad-thai.jpg

Until a photo is added, the app shows a neat gray placeholder box.
You can add photos any time — one by one is fine.
